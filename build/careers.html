Company Perks

At Half Helix, we strive for few job titles, few departments, and few hierarchies. We prefer composition of roles necessary for projects and company objectives over inheritance of bosses and direct reports. We don’t work on a fixed schedule or from a single fixed location We are at Half Helix primarily for our design and development skill, and want to apply it, rather than creating company overhead.
